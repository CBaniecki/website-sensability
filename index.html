Combining Music Therapy and Accessibility

Sensability strives to accomodate a music experience to Deaf and Hard of Hearing audiences, while integrating music as therapy. Currently, music instruments are not integrated with captions in songs. This app communicates the instruments while becoming (a music visualization which conveys the message of the song).

In addition to accessibility settings, you can choose the music visualizations by:

selecting music genres

selecting desired tempo of music

Choose your accessibility settings

Integrated accessibility settings (at the begining of this process) allow the user to enjoy an accomodating music experience, by selecting desired settings which assists with the music visualization selection.

The goal for Medo by Sensability is to maintain a tangible association with music, which can improve the cognitive function through touch. The textures establish an association to the notes in the music alongside the music visualization.

Sensability assists accomodations to music therapy as an (alternative) service. (With/By) subscribing, there is an (option/opportunity) to connect with a music therapist.
