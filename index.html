Combining Music Therapy and Accessibility

Sensability strives to accomodate a music experience to Deaf and Hard of Hearing audiences, while integrating music as therapy. Currently, music instruments are not integrated with captions in songs. This app communicates the instruments in a music visualization which conveys the message of the song.

Selection Process

The music visualization process is simple and easy, to get started:

select your desired music genres

select your desired tempo of music

Accessibility with music visualizations

Integrated accessibility settings at the begining of this process allow the user to enjoy an accomodating music experience, by selecting desired settings which assists with the music visualization selection.

Medo by Sensability

The goal for Medo by Sensability is to maintain a tangible association with music, which can improve the cognitive function through touch. The textures establish an association to the notes in the music alongside the music visualization.

Sensability assists accomodations to music therapy as an alternative service. By subscribing, there is an opportunity to connect with a music therapist.
